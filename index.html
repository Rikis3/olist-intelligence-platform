75% Drop in Repeat Purchases After Late Delivery

Strategic Recommendations

Data-driven action items to reduce revenue leakage, improve CSAT, and increase Customer Lifetime Value.

Revenue Impact

Estimated Annual Revenue Leakage: $2.4M

Based on root cause analysis, logistics-driven churn costs the marketplace $2.4M/year in lost repeat purchases and customer lifetime value.

Root Cause

60% of SLA Breaches Originate at Seller Dispatch

The majority of delays are NOT from carriers — sellers take >4 days to hand off packages. The carrier leg meets SLA in 87% of cases.

1

Implement Seller Dispatch Penalty System

Penalize sellers who consistently fail 48-hour dispatch SLAs. This alone could reduce overall SLA breaches by ~35%, directly improving CSAT and repeat rates.

Est. Impact: +2.1pp CSAT, -$840K leakage recovered
2

Open Northeast Cross-Docking Fulfillment Hub

Establish a secondary hub in Bahia for the North/Northeast corridor. Cuts average lead times from 21 → ~12 days and reduces freight costs by 18%.

Est. Impact: -6 day lead time, +$1.1M LTV recovered
3

Launch Win-Back Campaign for Churned Late-Delivery Customers

Target users who experienced an SLA breach in the last 6 months with a 15% discount win-back campaign. Could recover ~12% of churned high-value customers.

Est. Impact: +3.2K customers recovered, +$460K annual revenue
