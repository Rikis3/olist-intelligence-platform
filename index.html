Repeat Purchase Rate: On-Time vs Late The Churn Pipeline

Critical Finding

75% Drop in Repeat Purchases After Late Delivery

Customers who receive their order after the estimated delivery date are 75% less likely to make a second purchase. On-time customers: 18.5% repeat rate. Late customers: 4.5% repeat rate.

Warning

1-Star Reviews Correlate with SLA Breaches

When delivery exceeds the estimated date, the probability of a 1-star review spikes by 62%. The Northeast region accounts for 38% of all 1-star reviews despite only 14% of order volume.

Opportunity

RFM Segmentation Reveals High-Value Segment

The top 8% of customers (RFM Score: Champions) generate 31% of total GMV. Targeted retention campaigns for this segment could recover an estimated $740K in annual LTV leakage.

Strategic Recommendations

Data-driven action items to reduce revenue leakage, improve CSAT, and increase Customer Lifetime Value.

Revenue Impact

Estimated Annual Revenue Leakage: $2.4M

Based on our root cause analysis, logistics-driven churn is costing the marketplace an estimated $2.4M per year in lost repeat purchases and customer lifetime value.

Root Cause

60% of SLA Breaches Originate at Seller Dispatch

The majority of delivery delays are not caused by carriers — they originate from sellers taking more than 4 days to hand off packages. The carrier leg meets SLA in 87% of cases.

1

Implement Seller Dispatch Penalty System

Penalize sellers who consistently fail 48-hour dispatch SLAs. This alone could reduce overall SLA breaches by an estimated 35%, directly improving CSAT scores and repeat purchase rates.

Est. Impact: +2.1pp CSAT, -$840K leakage recovered
2

Open Northeast Cross-Docking Fulfillment Hub

Establish a secondary fulfillment hub in Bahia to service the North/Northeast corridor. This would cut average lead times from 21 days to ~12 days and reduce freight costs by 18%.

Est. Impact: -6 day lead time, +$1.1M LTV recovered
3

Launch Win-Back Campaign for Churned Late-Delivery Customers

Target users who experienced an SLA breach in the last 6 months with a 15% discount win-back campaign. Based on cohort data, this could recover approximately 12% of churned high-value customers.

Est. Impact: +3.2K customers recovered, +$460K annual revenue
